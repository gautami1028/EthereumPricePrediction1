Ethereum is the community-run technology powering the cryptocurrency ether (ETH) and thousands of decentralized applications.

What is Ethereum
Ethereum is a technology that's home to digital money, global payments, and applications. The community has built a booming digital economy, bold new ways for creators to earn online, and so much more. It's open to everyone, wherever you are in the world – all you need is the internet.
Key Use Cases
Ethereum expands blockchain tech with faster transactions, enhancing efficiency and decentralization. Key use cases include De-Fi, DAOs, Smart Contracts, NFTs, and Dapps. From entertainment to healthcare, ETH provides value across sectors, driven by its unique model.
